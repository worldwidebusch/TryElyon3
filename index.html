06.5 / FAQStraight answers

Questions owners actually ask.

How fast can my AI Agent go live?

Most agents go live 3 to 5 business days after you send your business information. Your 7-day free trial starts the day your agent is activated, not before.

Will it sound robotic to my customers?

No. Your agent is configured with your services, prices and tone of voice, and it hands the conversation to a human whenever a case needs one. Most customers simply experience fast, helpful answers.

Which languages does it speak?

Spanish and English, switching naturally based on how the customer writes or speaks.

Do I need a special WhatsApp account?

We set everything up for you on the official WhatsApp Business Platform. Your number stays yours, and your team can keep using it alongside the agent.

What happens after the 7-day trial?

You continue only if you want to. No contracts, cancel anytime. The setup fee applies only if you decide to keep your agent. And paid plans carry a 30-day guarantee: if your agent books no appointments in your first paid month, we refund it.

Is the free trial really free? What is included?

Yes. No card required. The trial includes up to 150 AI conversations and 30 voice minutes over 7 days, which is more than enough to see your agent reply, qualify and book with real customers. If you go past the included usage during the trial, we simply pause the agent and ask if you want to continue on a paid plan.

What counts as a conversation, and what if I go over my limit?

A conversation is one customer chat session within a 24-hour window, no matter how many messages it contains. If you pass your monthly limit, extra conversations are billed at the overage rate shown in the pricing section. We alert you at 80% of your limit so there are never surprises, and you can upgrade plans at any time.

Is my customer data safe?

Conversations run over encrypted channels and we store only what is needed to operate your agent. You can request deletion of your data at any time.
